About Krisha

Krisha Dubey, a 9-year-old storyteller from Singapore, is known for her creativity and love for writing. Alongside her passion for storytelling, she consistently excels academically, having won numerous awards at her school. Krisha’s vibrant imagination brings her stories to life, captivating young readers with fun adventures and memorable characters. Her ability to craft meaningful and exciting tales makes her an emerging talent in children’s literature.

Krisha's storytelling is rooted in creativity and warmth, making her stories a favorite among young readers and their families. With a natural talent for weaving fun and meaningful narratives, she is poised to become a beloved name in children's literature.
